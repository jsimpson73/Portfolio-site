Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process for a new project?

My process typically involves an initial consultation to understand your requirements, followed by research and planning. I then create wireframes and mockups for approval before proceeding to development. Throughout the process, I maintain regular communication and provide updates on progress.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality could take 8-12 weeks or more. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, I need to understand your project goals, target audience, desired features and functionality, design preferences, and timeline. The more details you can provide, the more accurate the quote will be. I'm happy to schedule a consultation to discuss your project in depth.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. I offer various maintenance packages to suit different needs and budgets.

Can you help with existing websites or only new ones?

I work with both new and existing websites. Whether you need a complete redesign, specific improvements, or troubleshooting for an existing site, I can help. I'll assess your current website and provide recommendations based on your goals and budget.
